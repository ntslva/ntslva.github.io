Nate Silva is a photographer living in Brooklyn, New York.
He has a background in both analog & digital photography in both 35mm & 120 formats. His work spans across various styles including portraiture, landscapes, street photography & abstract compositions.
You can reach him either by email or by reaching out on Instagram.
If you're looking for something to listen to, he has dj sets that you can find on Mixcloud.
